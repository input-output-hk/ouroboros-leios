Linear Leios Tuner

An interactive calculator for ballparking best-possible EB success rate and overall Leios capacity for various values of L and MaxSize, where L=3×Lhdr + Lvote + Ldiff.

Arrival rate of inter-RB gaps ≥ L (at 5% active slot coefficient)

Repeatedly toss a coin biased 19:1 toward heads (p = P(H) = 19/20, q = P(T) = 1/20). The renewal process jumps each time a tails terminates a run of ≥ L heads. Plot the PMF of the holding times (how many tosses between consecutive jumps). Rug plot of illustrative quantiles.

Theoretical average capacity (100% stake participating, every EB full & successful if L gap)

For each run length k from 2 to ⌈L×4÷3⌉, plots MaxSize divided by the mean holding time of the renewal process defined by k (which is MaxSize · q · pk). The step at k = L is highlighted.
